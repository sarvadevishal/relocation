International Flight Rescheduling Guide: Emirates Airlines

You initially booked flights with **Emirates from Orlando (MCO) to Hyderabad for August 30th.** With your relocation to California, you now need to reschedule these flights to depart from **San Francisco (SFO)**, with two adults flying to Hyderabad and one adult to Bangalore. Here's how to navigate this change:

Step-by-Step Guide for Emirates Flight Changes:

Contact Emirates Directly

Reach out to Emirates customer service or your travel agent immediately. Have your original booking reference, passenger names, and desired new dates/routes ready. They are the primary contact for modifications.

Confirm New Itinerary

Clearly state the new departure airport (SFO) and the split destinations (Hyderabad for 2 adults, Bangalore for 1 adult). Be flexible with dates if possible, as this can affect availability and cost.

Understand Potential Costs

Emirates' policies vary by fare class. Be prepared for: Change Fees (if applicable per your fare rules) and Fare Difference (if the new SFO-India flights are more expensive).

Receive Confirmation

Once changes are processed, ensure you receive new e-tickets with the updated itinerary and payment details. Verify all passenger names, dates, times, and destinations carefully.

Cost Analysis: Rescheduling vs. Rebooking

It's vital to compare the total cost of rescheduling your existing Emirates tickets (change fees + fare difference) versus canceling them and buying entirely new tickets from SFO. Sometimes, purchasing new tickets can be more economical.

Reschedule Existing Emirates Tickets

Cost Implications: Emirates Change Fee (varies by fare class, potentially $0 - $400+) + Fare Difference (if SFO-India flight price is higher than original MCO-Hyderabad).

Recommendation: Preferable if your original fare allows for low change fees and the new SFO flights are not significantly more expensive. Simpler process.

Cancel & Rebook New Tickets

Cost Implications: Potential Emirates Cancellation Fee (if applicable) + Full Cost of New SFO-Hyderabad/Bangalore Tickets. Original ticket value might be lost or issued as future flight credit.

Recommendation: Consider if rescheduling fees are very high, or if new, competitive fares are available that make rebooking more cost-effective overall.

Important: Emirates' specific fare rules are key. Always check your original ticket terms and directly compare quotes for rescheduling vs. new purchases. Don't forget any potential travel insurance you might have!
